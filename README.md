## Problem Statement
Environmental factors such as air pollution, extreme heat, and poor wind circulation have a direct and serious impact on public health.  
Existing weather and air-quality apps mainly **display raw data**, but they often fail to:
- Detect sudden hazardous events in real time
- Correlate multiple environmental factors together
- Explain *what the data actually means* for human health

This creates a gap between **data availability** and **actionable public health awareness**.

---

## Solution Overview
Green Health Monitor is a **real-time environmental monitoring and alert system** that:
- Continuously ingests live environmental data
- Detects dangerous public health conditions in real time
- Explains health risks using a local AI model in simple language
